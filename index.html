CA Maker

CA Maker allows you to build and simulate your own elementary cellular automaton. A cellular automaton (CA) is a row of cells in a particular state (in this case, black or white). Over time, the state of a cell may change, depending on the state of its neighbors, according to a set of rules.

There are 256 ways for a CA such as this one to behave, and they're numbered 0-255. Learn more about cellular automata rules, or change the rule by making a selection below. (Hint: Be sure to try rules 30, 110, and 190.)
