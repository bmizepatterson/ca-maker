In the animation below, each row represents one "generation" of a cellular automaton. There are 256 ways different rules that govern the way such a CA can change from one generation to the next, and they're numbered 0-255. Change the rule by making a selection below, or just click "Start" to see what Rule 90 is like. You can also add each new generation one at a time by clicking "Next Generation."

Some rules produce more interesting patterns than others. Be sure to try rules 62, 102, 149, and 225. Can you find any other interesting ones?
