CA Maker allows you to build and simulate your own elementary cellular automaton. A cellular automaton (CA) is a group of cells in a particular state (in this case, black or white). Over time, the state of a cell may change, depending on the state of its neighbors, according to a set of rules.

CA Maker uses the p5.js JavaScript library.

To learn more about implementing cellular automata in code, check out The Nature of Code, by Daniel Shiffman.
